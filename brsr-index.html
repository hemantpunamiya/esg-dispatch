About this index

India's BRSR Core assurance mandate widens to the top 500 listed companies in FY26 (it has covered the top 150 since FY24 and the top 250 since FY25, reaching the top 1,000 in FY27). As that perimeter widens, FY 2025–26 BRSRs are now landing on the exchanges in volume. This page tracks them in one place — company, filing date, sector, headline FY26 disclosures, and assurance provider — with a link to each filing summary and to the authoritative NSE master directory.

The table is sorted newest-first. Click any column header to re-sort. The authoritative master list — with downloadable PDFs and XBRL submissions — is maintained by NSE at nseindia.com/companies-listing/corporate-filings-bussiness-sustainabilitiy-reports.
